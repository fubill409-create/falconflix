ABOUT · PLATFORM

An AI + Web3 short‑drama ecosystem built for MENA

FalconFlix combines short‑form drama production, a streaming platform, tokenised consumption and community governance into a single stack, creating a new way for audiences, creators and investors to interact with stories.

Shorts Hub

A growing library of Chinese–Arabic premium short dramas spanning urban legends, luxury lifestyles, cross‑cultural romance, suspense and sci‑fi.

EGC Token System

Users spend EGC to unlock dramas, purchase digital goods and NFTs, while engagement rewards are also distributed in EGC, forming a closed‑loop content economy.

RWA Funding Layer

Each drama is structured as a 500k USD RWA pool. Investors subscribe using EGC and receive on‑chain claim tokens linked to that IP’s future revenue.

Community & DAO

Token holders participate in governance – voting on slates, priorities and experiments – with a clear roadmap towards a DAO‑driven content ecosystem.

RWA · STRUCTURE

Treating each short drama as a priced, fractionalised real‑world asset

Building on the proven economics of China’s micro‑drama market, FalconFlix structures each series as a discrete asset whose funding, ownership and cashflow distribution are recorded on‑chain via smart contracts.

Underlying assets

Drama IP focused on MENA stories – urban myths, high‑end lifestyle, cross‑cultural romance, adventure and sci‑fi – with each series designed for 10+ episodes.

Financial layer

Investors subscribe using major stablecoins and receive EGC plus title‑specific claim tokens (NFTs), representing their share of future revenue.

Distribution layer

Revenues come from app and web streaming, 3rd‑party platforms, advertising, product placement, IP licensing and selected offline activations.

EAGLECOIN · TOKENOMICS

EagleCoin: the economic backbone of FalconFlix

EagleCoin (EGC) is a utility token used for drama access, in‑app purchases, community incentives and governance. The total supply is capped at 1 billion, with no further issuance, and a clear allocation and vesting model.

Key parameters

· Name: EagleCoin (EGC)
· Network: Ethereum Layer2 (Polygon / Arbitrum)
· Total supply: 1,000,000,000 EGC
· Initial valuation: $100M (0.10 USDT / EGC)

Allocation

· Private & fundraising: 40% (400M)
· Community & content incentives: 35% (350M)
· Team & advisors: 15% (150M, ≥2‑year vesting)
· DAO reserve: 10% (100M)

Vesting

The first 300M EGC in the private round unlocks after a 3‑month cliff, then vests at 10% per month over 10 months. Team & advisor tokens vest linearly over a longer horizon for long‑term alignment.

FAQ · TOKEN VS. EQUITY

Aligning founders and investors in a dual‑track model

Does EGC represent company equity?

No. EGC is a functional platform token used for consumption and governance. Equity in the operating entity remains with shareholders and is not represented by EGC.

Investors hold 40% of tokens – who controls the project?

Governance is designed with vesting and distributed voting power to prevent a single dominant holder. The founding team retains strategic control through the DAO framework and corporate equity.

How do token and equity financing work together?

Token financing bootstraps users and market value, which then serves as a valuation anchor for future VC equity rounds. Founders benefit from both token appreciation and the rising value of the underlying company.
